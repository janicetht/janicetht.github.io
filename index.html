    About Our Project

    SEO is the process of optimizing a website or web page to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). The goal of SEO is to attract more organic traffic to a website and increase its visibility, which seeks more attention from the target audience. Google's PageRank algorithm is the most widely used algorithm for ranking web pages in SERPs.

    The PageRank algorithm assigns a score to each web page based on the number and quality of other web pages that link to it. The score is used to determine the importance and relevance of the web page to the user's search query.

    The PageRank algorithm equation is:

    where C(t) refers to the out-degree of t, α refers to the probability of random jump, N refers to the total number of nodes in the graph. However, there are some methods that can utilize this algorithm to pushing some irrelevant page onto the top. In this proposal, we will discuss methods to increase the importance score of a homepage. We will also propose some ideas for Google to counter the measures used to artificially improve the score of an arbitrary page and prevent its PageRank scheme from being abused.
